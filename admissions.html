ADMISSIONS

Students are eligible to attend The Montessori School of Washington if they reach their second birthday before August 31.

The curriculum is designed for children from two through six years of age, and all age groups are mixed in the classroom.

A non-refundable application fee of $60 is required to accompany the application.

In accordance with the Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, it is the Montessori School of Washington's policy to admit children without regard to race, color, or national origin.

A $350 non-refundable registration fee, submitted along with an Enrollment Form and D.C. Health Form, is required to secure your child's space in the program. Enrollment forms must be received by August 1.

For parents needing to drop off children before 9:00 am, The Montessori School of Washington offers before-school care beginning at 8:15am. Parents must designate the number of days per week they require before-school care in advance. The extra charge for this service ($110/month) will be billed in advance for the semester.

For parents who require occasional late pick-ups, after-school care is available for $10 per hour, payable upon pickup unless other arrangements have been made.

In case of illness or planned absences such as family vacations, The Montessori School of Washington does not issue credit days or refunds. We also request that you notify the School as soon as a child's inability to attend is established.
