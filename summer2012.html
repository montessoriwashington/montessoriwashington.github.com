The twelve weeks program will highlight various activities to include Dance, Music, Fun-Fitness, Out-Door Playtime, Sports (soccer), Art & Craft, Yoga, Cooking, Pottery, Fieldtrips, to name a few. Our school is Montessori based, however, our summer camp revolves more around learning through fun in a safe and supportive environment.

Specialist instruction from our friends at Joy of Motion (Dance) and My Gym (Fun Fitness) will be integrated in our weekly curriculum.

(Remind us to issue you your passports upon arrival)

General Camp Information

IMPORTANT DATES:
MSOW offers 12 weeks of summer fun from 4 June to 24 August. We will follow DC public school's schedule for holidays and weather.
There will be no camp on 4 July.

MSOW CAMP HOURS:
Early drop off: 8:15-9:00am ($10/hr.)
Camp day: 9:00am-12:00pm
               9:00am-3:00pm
After-care: 3:00-6:00pm ($10/hr.)

DROP OFF:
Our drop off location during camp sessions is: Hardy Park Corner of Q St. & Foxhall road
* Drop off will be at MSOW on rainy days.

REGISTRATION AND DEPOSIT:
To reserve a space in MSOW camp, please complete the registration form and return to us with a $200 deposit for each session (week) you are registering for by April 30th.
* Payment may be made by cash or check.
* The balance of camp fees, all camp forms and an up-to-date health form are due the first day your child starts camp.

REFUNDS:
No refunds will be made for absences or withdrawals during the camp session.

FINAL CONFIRMATION:
Please note that your child's registration is not final until you receive a confirmation email from msow.dc@gmail.com and all camp payments and documents have been received.
